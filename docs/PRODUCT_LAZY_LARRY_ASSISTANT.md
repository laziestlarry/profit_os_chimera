# Lazy Larry Personal Assistant - Desktop Software & SaaS
**Multi-layer Profiled AI Assistant**

---

## Product Overview

**Name:** Lazy Larry Personal Assistant  
**Tagline:** "Your AI Assistant That Actually Gets You"

A desktop application and SaaS platform featuring a multi-layer profiled AI assistant that understands your professional, financial, personal, behavioral, and contextual needs.

---

## Features

### Multi-Layer Profiling
- **Professional Layer:** Work, skills, career goals
- **Financial Layer:** Income, expenses, financial goals
- **Personal Layer:** Interests, preferences, lifestyle
- **Behavioral Layer:** Patterns, habits, work style
- **Contextual Layer:** Current situation, immediate needs

### Capabilities
- Task management and automation
- Income opportunity scouting
- Content creation assistance
- Emotional support and motivation
- Proactive suggestions
- Chat interface
- Desktop integration
