# Opportunity Hunter Service - Productized Offer
**Automated Income Opportunity Scouting & Application**

---

## Product Overview

**Name:** Opportunity Hunter by AutonomaX  
**Tagline:** "Never Miss an Income Opportunity Again"

An automated service that scouts income opportunities (freelance, remote jobs, side hustles, passive income) and automatically applies to high-match opportunities.

---

## What It Does

1. **Automated Scouting**
   - Scans Fiverr, Upwork, RemoteOK, job boards
   - Identifies opportunities matching your profile
   - Scores and ranks opportunities

2. **Auto-Application**
   - Generates application materials
   - Submits applications automatically
   - Tracks application status

3. **Income Stream Analysis**
   - Analyzes current income streams
   - Identifies optimization opportunities
   - Suggests diversification strategies
